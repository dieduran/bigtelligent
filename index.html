Experts in data solutions

We are a team of professional data specialists who formed BIGTELLIGENT to provide our clients with reliability in the use of the information they have at their disposal as a result of their business.

We offer customized developments for integration and data movement, including the implementation and maintenance of the environments where they are supported.

We specialize in Microsoft platform products, On-Premises and Cloud. We work on Azure Data Factory, Power BI, SQL Server, Integration Services, Analysis Services, Power Platform, Reporting Services and TimeXtender tools as well as SQL server solutions in cloud (Managed Instancies, Azure SQL databases) and on premises.
